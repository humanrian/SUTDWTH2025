PillRemind, Designed by Humans

Solution for Independent Elderly Care

Our system is a solution designed to give caregivers the flexibility to manage medication schedules, monitor adherence, and improve patient safety.

Problem Context

Older adults often forget to take their medicine on time, which can affect their health. Current reminder tools are frequently difficult to use or lack personalisation. Many older adults also feel uncomfortable depending on caregivers for such routine tasks, as it affects their sense of independence. A simple and easy reminder system is needed to help them take medicine on time while stayingÂ independent.

Our Solution

Automated Reminders Module

The system makes voice calls in multiple languages to remind patients to take their medicine. This can be paried with hardware that has light, buzzer, and vibration alerts.

Pill Dispenser Module

In future development, we can create a modular pill dispenser system that can hold up to 10 different types of pills, allowing for flexible medication management. The dispenser is designed for the most common medication types given to patients.

Web-Based Control Module

Caregivers can easily track and manage medication schedules, patient engagement, and dispenser status from a simple website interface.

How It Works

Our solution streamlines the entire medication process for both caregivers and patients:

Weekly Setup

For hardware, staff or helpers load each of the 10 compartments with up to a week's worth of medication, aligning with typical care routines.

Scheduled Alerts

At the set time, the pill reminder system will give the care receiver a call to remind them to take their medication.

Real-Time Notifications

Caregivers and family members are instantly updated on whether a dose was taken or missed, ensuring timely intervention.
